The Promise of Consultative Selling

After speaking with Pavel, I understood that NICE CX operates on consultative selling — not just transactional. That's exactly the style of selling I want to grow into, and that promise of mentorship is a big reason I'm excited about this opportunity.

Why Consultative Selling Matters to Me

Let me share a simple story that shaped how I think about sales.

❌ Scenario A

The doctor gives 2 minutes. Orders tests. Prescribes tablets. No explanation. You leave wondering — is he helping me, or just making money?

✅ Scenario B

The doctor gives 10 minutes. Explains the condition clearly. Walks through the plan. You leave with trust — and you follow every word.

That's how I see sales. First understand the pain. Then consult. Then offer the right solution. NICE CX is the right place for that journey.
